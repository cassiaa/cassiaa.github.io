A community of hundreds of millions of self-selected creatives.

Including you.

An all-inclusive, extensive platform

Get inspired by work shared by citizens across the entire globe. Gain access to a world-wide audience for free. As a member of the Society of Independent Citizens, the world is your oyster.

Sans jury, ni récompense

Unlike galleries and museums, our community members are the only ones who select the pieces to display in our exhibitions. Display and price your work exactly how you want; you have control over your own work.

Every step, made easy

Cultivate your creative identity without putting yourself through the art-world wringer. You don't need an MFA or an exhibition at the Guggenheim to be an artist; our platform makes the artistic pipeline, from inspiration and creation to distribution and recognition, accessible to everyone.
